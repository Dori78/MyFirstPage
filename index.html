How to make tea

Tea is flavourful way of getting enough fluid into your body each day. On top of that, studies have shown teas can help protect your teeth and heart.

Ingredients

  • Tea bag
  • Water
  • Milk(optional)
  • Sugar or honey

Preparation

  1. Boil a tap water
  2. Pop a tea bag in a big mug
  3. Pour the hot water over
  4. Leave the tea bag to release flavour for 3-4 min and remove
  5. You can throw some milk now if you like
  6. Sweeten with sugar or honey
  7. Enjoy!
